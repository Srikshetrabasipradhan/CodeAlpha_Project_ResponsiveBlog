Apollo Bandar, Colab, Mumbai, Maharastra 400001

India Gate is one of the most famous buildings of Mumbai and the city symbol. It was built to commemorate the visit of King George V of England in 1911 on the waterfront, next to the port, in order to be the first thing that the boats saw when they were arriving in the country.

Jagannth Temple, Puri, Odisha

The Jagannath Puri temple is a sacred temple devoted to Lord Jagannath along with his brother Lord Balabhadra and sister Devi Subhadra. Lord Jagannath is considered to be a form of Lord Vishnu by Hindus. The Jagannath Temple is one of the four pilgrimage sites of Char Dham Pilgrimage by the Hindu population.

Amritsar, Amritsar Cantt, Punjab

Literally made of gold, this Gurudwara has been built around a man-made pool that is known to have powerful healing properties. Once you enter the complex, you will experience inner peace like never before. Amritsar's Swarn Mandir is one place that celebrates spiritual awakening in the best possible way.

Kedarnath, Uttarakhand

Kedarnath Temple is one of the most sacred temples and the holiest Hindu pilgrimages in India. A part of Chhota Char Dham Yatra in Uttarakhand, Kedarnath is the most revered among the 12 Jyotirlingas in India dedicated to Lord Shiva. Devotees believe that a visit to this temple can free them from the cycle of life. The inner walls of the temple are carved with figures of various deities and scenes from mythology.
